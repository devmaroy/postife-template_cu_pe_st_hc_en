About Us

So where should we start?

For the first time everything started when I realized there was no easy place to save pictures. I sat down, began to think, began to write code. Postife was born. It was not easy to say the truth, but it was worth it.

Over time, Postife has become a popular place for storing pictures and various files. Postife is about simplicity, anonymity and easy access to things. We love simplicity and therefore our design matches it.

Our team is ready to answer any questions in the contact section. We are glad you are with us.
